Ingredients of Butter Chicken for 4-5 persons


Chicken - 1 kg
Water - 1 cup
Oil
Ginger - 100 g
Garlic - 8-9 cloves
Onions - 4 medium sized
Tomato - 10 medium sized
Cashunuts - 10-12
Green Chilli - 2-3
Butter - 100 g
Kasoori Methi - 2 tea spoons
Cream - 1 tbsp
Vinegar - 1/2 tea spoon

Spices :
Salt - According to your taste
Suger - 1/2 tea spoon
Red Chilli Powder
Corriander Powder
Garam Masala
Chicken Masala
